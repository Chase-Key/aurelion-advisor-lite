## üéì How I Built This Hub

**Timeline:**
- **Q3 2023:** Started with just Daily Operations checklist (simple to-do list)
- **Q4 2023:** Added Career Master after missing out on promotion (needed clarity on goals)
- **Q1 2024:** Skills Inventory added when manager asked "what do you want to learn?"

- **Current:** Integrated system, ~12 active documents, daily habit

**Philosophy:**
- **Start small:** Don't try to build entire system at once
- **Solve problems:** Add files when you have a need (not preemptively)
- **Iterate:** Hub evolved over time based on what I actually used
- **Consistency > Perfection:** Better to update Daily Operations every day (even quick notes) than skip because "I don't have time to do it perfectly"

## üìñ Lessons Learned

### What Works
‚úÖ **Daily Operations is my anchor** - If I only updated one file, this is it. Keeps me focused.  
‚úÖ **Career Master clarity** - Having 5-year vision written down = massive confidence boost. I know where I'm going.  
‚úÖ **Skills Inventory for development discussions** - Manager loves that I come to 1:1s with specific learning plan.  
‚úÖ **Project Investigation template** - Churn Analysis would have taken 2x longer without structured methodology.  
‚úÖ **Weekly reviews are non-negotiable** - This habit alone = 50% of hub value. Keeps everything current.

### What I Adjusted
üîÑ **Stopped using:** Glossary (didn't need it‚Äîterminology was easy to remember)  
üîÑ **Combined files:** Merged "Meeting Notes" into Communication Log (less redundant)  
üîÑ **Simplified templates:** Original templates were too detailed‚ÄîI streamlined to what I actually fill out  
üîÑ **Archive old projects:** Moved 2023 projects to separate file so My Hub isn't cluttered

### Mistakes to Avoid
‚ùå **Don't over-engineer:** I initially had 25 files‚Äîtoo many, couldn't maintain  
‚ùå **Don't wait for perfection:** Just start typing‚Äîstructure emerges with use  
‚ùå **Don't skip reviews:** Went 6 weeks without weekly review once‚Äîhub became outdated, lost value  
‚ùå **Don't copy someone else's system:** This hub is MINE (works for my role, career). Yours should be different.
