## ğŸ›ï¸ The 5-Floor Architecture

ADVISOR organizes your knowledge like a circular library with 5 floors:

### **Floor 1: Foundation & Navigation (Lore & History)**
*Your operational foundation*

**What lives here:**
- Career planning & advancement roadmap
- Skills inventory & competencies
- Daily operations & responsibilities
- Past project case studies

**Example files:**
- `career-master.md` - Track goals, promotion criteria, evidence
- `skills-inventory.md` - Technical skills, certifications, proficiency levels
- `daily-operations.md` - Recurring tasks, monitoring routines
- `project-archive/` - Past investigations and learnings

---

### **Floor 2: Systems & Understanding (Magic & Aetherstone)**
*Your frameworks and mental models*

**What lives here:**
- Investigation methodologies
- Communication templates & standards
- Quality criteria & checklists
- Learning frameworks

**Example files:**
- `investigation-template.md` - Reusable problem-solving framework
- `communication-standards.md` - Email templates, documentation formats
- `qa-standards.md` - Your criteria for "done right"
- `glossary.md` - Domain terminology

### **Floor 3: Networks & Relationships (People & Politics)**
*Your stakeholder map and strategic positioning*

**What lives here:**
- Organizational network maps
- Stakeholder relationship tracking

- `stakeholder-map.md` - Who matters, communication strategies

*Your tactical playbooks and execution guides*

### First 30 Minutes: Quick Setup

**Step 1: Hub Index (5 minutes)**
- Open `00_Hub_Index.md`
- Fill in "Most Used" section with links to your top 3 templates
- You can refine this later - start simple

**Step 2: Career Master (10 minutes)**
- Open `01_Career_Master.md`
- Answer these minimum questions:
  - What role do I have now?
  - What role do I want in 1 year?
  - What's my biggest career goal for this quarter?
- Leave the rest blank for now

**Step 3: Skills Inventory (10 minutes)**
- Open `02_Skills_Inventory.md`
- List your top 5 technical skills
- Rate each: Developing / Proficient / Advanced / Expert
- Note 1 skill you want to improve this quarter

**Step 4: Daily Operations (5 minutes)**
- Open `03_Daily_Operations.md`
- List 3 recurring tasks you do weekly/monthly
- Note today's top 3 priorities
- This becomes your daily touchpoint

**âœ… Done!** You now have a functional foundation. Everything else can be added as needed.

## â“ FAQ

**Q: Is this just fancy note-taking?**  
A: No! ADVISOR is structured methodology documentation. It's the difference between random notes and an organized library with clear architecture.

**Q: Do I need all 5 floors?**  
A: Start with Floor 1-2 (Foundation + Frameworks). Add floors as complexity grows.

**Q: Can I customize the structure?**  
A: Absolutely! Templates are starting points. Adapt to your domain.

**Q: How is this different from Obsidian/Notion?**  
A: ADVISOR provides the *structure* (5-floor framework). Use it with Obsidian/Notion/VS Code/any tool you want!

**Q: I'm not technical - is this for me?**  
A: Yes! If you can edit Word docs, you can use ADVISOR. It's just markdown files.

**Q: How long to set up?**  
A: 30 minutes for basics, 1-2 hours for comprehensive setup, ongoing 15-30 min/week maintenance.
