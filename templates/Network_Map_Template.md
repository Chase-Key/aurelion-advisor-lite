## Network Health Indicators

**Healthy Network Signs:**
- Regular, natural communication with key contacts
- Mutual value exchange
- Diverse connections across domains/industries
- New opportunities emerging from connections
- Strong trust levels with core relationships

**Red Flags:**
- One-sided relationships (always asking, never giving)
- Dormant relationships with key strategic contacts
- Lack of diversity in network
- Communication feels forced or transactional
- No new connections formed in 6+ months
